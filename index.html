Align Your Clinical Decisions With What Patients Want

EMGENEX combines PGX testing with other practice and data management services to take your practice farther than it’s ever been. Welcome to EMGENEX. We’re pioneering the combination of high-impact diagnostic services with smarter business operations.

As the healthcare industry moves into a new era of technology, the importance of reliable, accurate, and complete data has never been more critical. At EMGENEX, when you ask what’s next for your practice and your patients, we have answers.

Today, more than 85% of patients have detectable variations in their DNA that can increase their risk for adverse drug events and an estimated $136 billion is spent annually on treating adverse drug events. EMGENEX is the company that can help you predict individual patient response and reaction to the drugs you prescribe for your patients, leading to reduced adverse events, reduced hospitalizations, improved outcomes, and increased patient compliance and confidence.

Better Medicine Is in Your Genes

Will you be able to tolerate a new medicine without side effects? Will it work? How many medicines will you have to try before you find one that works?

A new era of technology that is revolutionizing how medicine is prescribed has emerged. EMGENEX, founded in 2009, is helping to lead that revolution.

Your doctor can administer a simple cheek swab saliva test right in the office. The results of this pharmacogenetics test (PGX) help your provider to prescribe medicines that work better and that you can tolerate better. This test means that your provider can avoid the trial and error of having you try several medications, each with the potential for side effects, before they find the right one. Now you can be on the right medicine right from the start.
